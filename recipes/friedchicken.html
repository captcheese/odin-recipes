Fried Chicken

Description

A classic and quick chicken dish. A family favourite.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 teaspoon paprika
  • 2.5 lb chicken legs or drumsticks
  • 4 tablespoon flour
  • 4 tablespoon fat, (lard and butter)
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon water

Steps

  1. Sprinkle each piece with salt and paprika, and roll in flour
  2. Add the fat to the frying pan
  3. When very hot add the chicken
  4. Allow all the pieces to brown thoroughly; cover the pan with a lid and add the water, lower the heat and cooke over medium heat for thirty minutes
  5. Turn frequently to prevent scorching
